# Goldstonian Concordance Bible Corpus

Author: Justin Goldston, PhD

## Corpus Overview

The Goldstonian Concordance Bible Corpus is a structured dataset representing relationships across biblical texts, thematic classifications, and interpretive commentary nodes.

The corpus is designed for use in digital humanities research, computational theology, and knowledge graph analysis.

The corpus includes:

- structured scripture references
- cross-reference relationships
- thematic mappings
- commentary nodes
- multimedia teaching links

## Corpus Structure

The corpus is organized around passage-level nodes defined by:

book  
chapter  
verse  

These nodes form the base unit for cross-references, themes, and commentary.

## Research Applications

The corpus may support research in:

- computational biblical studies
- digital humanities
- knowledge graph construction
- natural language processing
- theological text analysis
