Methodology

The dataset was constructed using a concordance-based mapping process.

1. Canon Identification
An expanded canon was defined for study.

2. Passage Segmentation
Texts are segmented by book, chapter, and verse.

3. Cross-Reference Mapping
Relationships between passages are recorded.

4. Theme Assignment
Passages are associated with thematic categories such as law, prophecy, wisdom, covenant, redemption, and eschatology.

5. Commentary Nodes
Interpretive commentary nodes are linked to passages.

6. Multimedia Indexing
Educational video explanations are associated with passages when available.

The dataset is designed to support computational exploration of scripture relationships.
